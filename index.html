How To Play This Game:

  • You will be shown a random number for "Match This Number" box at the start of the game. This number will range between 19 and 120.
  • There are 4 crystals below. Each crystal holds different value between 1 and 12. By clicking on a crystal, you will add that hidden, assigned points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number exactly. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.
  • New random values are generated once again for each crystals as well as the "Match this Number" field for each new game.
